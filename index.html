Welcome to The Gourd Gals' pumpkin patch

Cuckoo for cucumbers? Smitten by squash? Partial to pumpkins? Mesmerized by melons? You're in the right place! Gourd Gals is North America's #1 Cucurbita fanclub. The organizers of the much celebrated annual Squash Convention & Jamboree ----- boasted among our membership a number of distinguished gals including the govenor general of Canada, Her Excellency The Right Honorable Mary Simon, famed talk show host and television producer Oprah Winfrey, and American novelist and playwright Cormac McCarthy (honorary gal and long-time gourd enthusiast).
